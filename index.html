Meet Dr. Sarah Mitchell, DDS

Dr. Sarah Mitchell earned her Doctor of Dental Surgery degree from the University of Texas School of Dentistry and has been serving the Austin community since 2010. With 14 years of experience in both general and cosmetic dentistry, Dr. Mitchell is known for her gentle chairside manner, her attention to detail, and her genuine care for every patient's long-term oral health.

She stays current with the latest dental techniques through continuing education and is a proud member of the American Dental Association and the Texas Dental Association. When she's not in the clinic, Dr. Mitchell volunteers her dental services at Austin community health events.
